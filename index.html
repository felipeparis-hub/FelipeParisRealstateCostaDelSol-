Zones & prices

Where to buy on
the Costa del Sol

🏙️
Málaga Capital
€2,200 – €4,500/m²

The up-and-coming urban investment. Strong short-term rental yields, growing international profile, direct airport access.

Best for: city apartments, rental income
🌴
Marbella / Puerto Banús
€4,000 – €15,000+/m²

The classic luxury destination. Villas, penthouses, world-class amenities. Strong UK, French and Arab buyer demand.

Best for: prestige assets, capital growth
🏖️
Torremolinos / Benalmádena
€1,600 – €3,000/m²

Best value on the coast. High holiday rental occupancy, direct beach access, well-connected transport links.

Best for: entry-level investment, first property
⛳
Estepona / Nueva Andalucía
€2,500 – €6,000/m²

The golf coast sweet spot. Newer developments, quieter lifestyle, strong French and Belgian buyer community.

Best for: golf properties, retirement, long-term rentals
