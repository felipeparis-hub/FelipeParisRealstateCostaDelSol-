Zones & Prices

Where to buy on the Costa del Sol

A snapshot of the 4 main investment zones I cover — with real price ranges as of 2025.

🏙️

Málaga Capital

€2,200 – €4,500/m²

The up-and-coming urban investment. Strong short-term rental yields, growing international profile, airport access. Best for: city apartments, rental income.

🌴

Marbella / Puerto Banús

€4,000 – €15,000+/m²

The classic luxury destination. Villas, penthouses, world-class amenities. Strong demand from UK, French, and Arab buyers. Best for: prestige assets and capital growth.

🏖️

Torremolinos / Benalmádena

€1,600 – €3,000/m²

Best value on the coast. High occupancy rates for holiday rentals, direct beach access, well-connected transport links. Best for: entry-level investment, first property.

⛳

Estepona / Nueva Andalucía

€2,500 – €6,000/m²

The golf coast sweet spot. Newer developments, quieter lifestyle, strong French and Belgian buyer community. Best for: golf properties, retirement, long-term rentals.
